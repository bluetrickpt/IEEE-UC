About IEEE-UC Student Branch


The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers or IEEE is the world's largest professional association. It's a non-profit organization whose main goal is to contribute to technology development worlwide. The IEEE was created to help every professional in electronics and compute sciences to keep up with the ongoing change of today's science and tecnology. http://www.ieee.org

The IEEE branch of Universidade de Coimbra was founded in the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering with the goal of helping every student to expand profissionally and personally. We seek to stimulate both healthy competition as well as cooperation and mutual aid by periodically doing practical challenges tecnology related. The development of social activities in order to stimulate interaction betewen members will also be in mind. The ideia behind the foundation of our branch was adjacent to the primordial ideia that the IEEE has to contribute through the share of knowledge to the progress of science and tecnology.
